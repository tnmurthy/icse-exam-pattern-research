## Key Research Findings

### Pre-2020 (2011–2020): Cognitive Level Distribution

| Subject | Knowledge % | Understanding % | Application % | Analysis % | Evaluation % | HOT % | LOT % |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mathematics | 5 | 10 | 75 | 8 | 2 | 10 | 90 |
| Physics | 15 | 20 | 50 | 12 | 3 | 15 | 85 |
| Chemistry | 35 | 30 | 25 | 8 | 2 | 10 | 90 |
| Biology | 30 | 40 | 15 | 12 | 3 | 15 | 85 |
| English Language | 10 | 30 | 25 | 25 | 10 | 35 | 65 |
| History & Civics | 40 | 35 | 15 | 8 | 2 | 10 | 90 |
| Geography | 35 | 30 | 20 | 12 | 3 | 15 | 85 |

### Post-2020 Reform Trajectory (2020–2025)

| Year | Key CISCE Action | HOT % Target |
|---|---|---|
| 2021 | COVID-era papers; no major change | ~10% |
| 2022 | NEP 2020 influence starts | ~12% |
| 2023 | CISCE circular issued for HOT increase | ~15% |
| 2024 | HOT increase officially implemented | ~20% |
| 2025 | Item Banks for competency-based Qs released | ~25% |
| 2026 | 40% competency-focused questions mandated | **40%** |

### Section Architecture

| Section | Type | Choice | Cognitive Level | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Section A | Short-answer, MCQ | No choice — all compulsory | Knowledge / Understanding | 40 |
| Section B | Long-answer, problem-solving | Choose 4 out of 6 | Application / Analysis | 40 |

### Official Difficulty Split

| Difficulty | % of Marks | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Easy | ~30% | Direct recall, formula substitution |
| Average | ~50% | 2–3 step reasoning, standard application |
| Difficult | ~20% | Multi-step, real-world context, HOT |
